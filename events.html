Term 2 Professional Learning for Stage 3 Teachers

We are planning to run a professional learning program for Stage 3 teachers over Term 2 this year. The program would involve weekly after-school sessions, 2 hours in duration, which will be held at a computer lab at the University of Newcastle's Callaghan Campus.

The program will involve:

  • an introduction to Coding and Computational Thinking
  • activities that demonstrate how to integrate these skills across the different Key Learning Areas
  • opportunities for self-reflection and collaboration with other teachers and university researchers

The program will be free and accredited with the New South Wales Education Standards Authority (NESA) for 30 hours of professional learning. The program will address the following standards at the Proficient level: 2.6.2, 3.3.2, 3.4.2, 4.5.2, 6.2.2, 6.4.2.

If you are interested in participating in the program, please complete the form available here. If you have any enquiries about the program, you can contact Daniel Hickmott through email (Daniel.Hickmott@uon.edu.au) or by phone (02 4921 8786) to find out more.
